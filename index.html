About Us

We are a group of UC Merced students who have recognized the lack of awareness and accessible resources of mental health issues in marginalized communities. We are motivated to turn this around and provide the necessary resources to those struggling with mental health issues. We hope that people find what they need on our website through mind exercises, self-help blogs, and resources nearby ranging from therapists to yoga spots to distress.
